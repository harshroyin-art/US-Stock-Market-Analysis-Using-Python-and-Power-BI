US Stock Market Analysis using Python & Power BI

📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes historical stock market data of leading US companies across multiple sectors using Python for data preprocessing and exploratory data analysis (EDA) and Power BI for interactive dashboard creation.

The objective is to uncover trends in stock prices, trading volume, daily returns, sector performance, and market volatility while providing an interactive dashboard for business users and investors.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze historical stock market performance (2020–2026).
Clean and preprocess stock market data using Python.
Calculate Daily Return Percentage.
Classify trading days into Bullish and Bearish.
Compare stock performance across different sectors.
Analyze stock price trends and trading volume.
Study moving averages for trend identification.
Build an interactive Power BI dashboard for business insights.

📌 Key Insights
Stock prices showed an overall upward trend from 2020 to 2026 despite periods of market volatility.
Healthcare and Financial Services sectors recorded some of the strongest long-term price growth.
Energy stocks experienced the highest intraday price fluctuations, indicating greater volatility.
Bullish and Bearish trading days were nearly equally distributed across the dataset.
Trading volume peaked during periods of significant market movement, suggesting increased investor activity.
The 50-day and 200-day moving averages effectively highlighted long-term market trends and potential trend reversals.
Open, High, Low, and Close prices exhibited very strong positive correlations, while trading volume had a weak negative relationship with price variables.
Sector-wise analysis revealed varying levels of volatility and performance, demonstrating the importance of diversification in investment strategies.
